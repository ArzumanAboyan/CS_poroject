How to play

This is a game of two!
There is only you and them!

Wait for your enemy! Then...
Put some powerful ships in your area in the Caribbean Sea and prepare to defend!

On your turn click somewhere on the Opponent's Sea and try to find and destroy your enemy's ships!

You have to find them ALL.

Good Luck, my Captain!
